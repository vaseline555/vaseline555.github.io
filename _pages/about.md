Seok-Ju has recently recevied Ph.D. (2024.08) in Industrial Engineering at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology ([UNIST](https://www.unist.ac.kr); Republic of Korea), advised by Prof. [Junghye Lee](https://d3mlab.snu.ac.kr/members/professor) and Prof. [Gi-Soo Kim](https://sdm.unist.ac.kr/members/). 
He received his B.S. (2019.02) and M.S. (2021.02) degrees in Industrial Engineering from UNIST.

He is now a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Engineering Research, Seoul National University ([IoER, SNU](https://ioer.snu.ac.kr); Republic of Korea).
Concurrently, he has been conscripted into the Republic of Korea Army as a [Technical Research Personnel](https://elaw.klri.re.kr/kor_service/jomunPrint.do?hseq=10572&cseq=235795) (started from 2022.09). (Date of Discharge: 2025.08)

His research interests include `Federated Learning`, `Collaborative Machine Learning`, and `Practical and Private Distributed Systems`.
Please see his [research statement](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1vtFEWnBLpo6Q2YfffwHVnzFnt7Ikv-m8/view?usp=drivesdk) for more details.
